About KOVA

KOVA Technologies is a next-generation tech company built on a singular belief — that technology should be powerful, intelligent, and seamless. We build systems that think, interfaces that feel alive, and software that works for people.

Founded by a young visionary developer, KOVA stands for Knowledge Oriented Vision & Architecture. Every product we ship reflects this philosophy — deliberate, purposeful, and built to last.

In Finnish, "Kova" means strong, powerful, hard. That is the standard we hold ourselves to.
